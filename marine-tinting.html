Why Marine Tint

Built for the Water

Life on a boat means constant sun exposure. Marine window film protects your vessel, your crew, and your investment.

🌊

Saltwater Resistant

Marine-grade adhesives and films engineered to withstand the corrosive effects of salt air, spray, and coastal conditions.

☀️

UV & Heat Protection

Block 99% of UV rays that damage gel coats, upholstery, electronics, and wood finishes below deck.

👁️

Glare Reduction

Water glare is blinding and dangerous. Tint dramatically improves visibility for captains and passengers.

🛥️

Cabin Comfort

Keep the cabin and helm station significantly cooler without over-relying on marine AC systems.

🔒

Privacy at Dock

Enjoy privacy when docked at the marina without covering your windows or closing blinds.

💎

Preserve Your Investment

UV and heat are the #1 destroyers of marine interiors. Film extends the life of everything inside your vessel.

Our Process

We Come to Your Vessel

No need to trailer your boat. We meet you at the marina, dock, or boatyard.

Consultation

Tell us about your vessel — make, model, glass types, and what you need. We'll recommend the right marine film.

On-Site Visit

We come to your vessel to measure, inspect glass, and provide a detailed quote on the spot.

Expert Install

Our team works directly on your boat with tools and techniques designed for curved marine glass and tight spaces.

Set Sail

Inspect the results, get your warranty info, and enjoy a cooler, more comfortable ride on the water.
